Year

Client

Personal Project

Services

Project

Online Sales

Description

I designed a user-friendly website for GHOMI Marvels Church, located in New Kahawa Sukari, Nairobi. The site was built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on showcasing the church’s mission to preach God’s Kingdom and provide essential information about church events, services, and ministries. The layout emphasizes simplicity and accessibility, making it easy for visitors to navigate and stay informed about the church’s activities.

The website also includes a dedicated section for online sermons, allowing members and visitors to access past teachings and stay spiritually engaged from anywhere. I implemented responsive design principles to ensure a seamless experience across devices, enhancing user interaction and accessibility. The project aimed to extend the church’s digital presence and improve communication within the congregation.
